Hints

  • Buy units (loggers, miners and soldiers) by clicking the buildings.
  • You must buy a mine before you can buy an army base.
  • Use the units to their strengths:
    • Loggers - GREEN - are cheap and will chop down trees for a little cash
    • Miners - GREY - are moderately priced and will mine ore for lots of cash
    • Soldiers - PURPLE - are expensive but will fight off enemies
  • Bear in mind that you will be up against:
    • Enemies - RED - will attack all of your units!
  • The trick of the game is the targeting system ;-)
